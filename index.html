I won't always be
in this shell, you know.
I've hatched a brilliant plan.

I'll push my way out
when the time is just right,
and see what sort of creature I am.

I bet I'll have fangs
and a looooooooooooooooong, slimy tail
that rattles when I get upset.

My three glowing eyes eyes eyes
will sit nicely beside
the horn growing of my head

I'll hang upside-down
dripping poisonous goo
from the tips of my eight, hairy legs.

I can't wait to see
just how scary I'll be
when I finally get out of this egg!
